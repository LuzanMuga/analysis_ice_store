# Video Game Market Analysis

## English Summary

Ice Store, an international video game retailer, needed to identify which factors were most strongly associated with commercial success in order to optimize marketing investments and prioritize products for the 2017 sales cycle.

Using historical sales data across multiple regions, platforms, genres, critic reviews, user reviews, and ESRB ratings, I analyzed market trends, regional consumer preferences, platform performance, and the relationship between product perception and sales outcomes.

The analysis revealed that platform selection, genre category, regional audience behavior, and critic reviews were among the strongest predictors of commercial performance.

## Key Achievements

* Identified PS4, Xbox One, and PC as the strongest commercial opportunities for the upcoming sales cycle.
* Discovered that Action, Shooter, and Sports were the highest-performing genres globally.
* Demonstrated that critic reviews had a stronger relationship with sales performance than user reviews.
* Uncovered significant differences in gaming preferences between North American, European, and Japanese markets.
* Developed market-specific recommendations to improve advertising efficiency and product prioritization.
* Applied statistical testing to validate business assumptions regarding platform and genre performance.

---

## Business Impact

The findings provided a framework for prioritizing advertising budgets, selecting high-potential product categories, and adapting marketing strategies to regional customer behavior.

By understanding the drivers of commercial success, Ice Store can make more informed decisions regarding inventory planning, campaign execution, and future market opportunities.


# Análisis del Mercado de Videojuegos

## Resumen

Ice Store, una cadena internacional de venta de videojuegos, necesitaba comprender qué características estaban asociadas con un mejor desempeño comercial para tomar decisiones más informadas sobre inventario, marketing y oportunidades de crecimiento.

A través del análisis de datos históricos de ventas, plataformas, géneros, reseñas de usuarios, reseñas de críticos y clasificaciones ESRB, evalué tendencias de mercado, preferencias regionales y factores relacionados con el éxito comercial de los videojuegos.

El análisis permitió identificar plataformas con mayor potencial, géneros dominantes y diferencias significativas en el comportamiento de los consumidores entre regiones.

Esto proporciona una base sólida para mejorar la eficiencia de las inversiones publicitarias y maximizar oportunidades de crecimiento.
